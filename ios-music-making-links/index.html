iOS music links – information, developer and other resources

Given the growing popularity of iOS as a music making platform, there is a corresponding growth in cool places online where you can find out more about mobile music technology, the best music apps, associated hardware or pick the brains of other iOS music producers.

This page collects some of the best links currently available and I’ll add more stuff as I find it. I’m bound to have missed plenty of sites/people/things (sorry!) so, if you know of a link that you think ought to be added here and want to share it with others in the mobile music making community, then please do let me know.
